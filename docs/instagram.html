CDL Instagram

The CDL instagram is currently run by Tara and is used as a more frequently updated public-facing platform. Lab members are encouraged to share any photos or videos of their work with Tara (or request access to our CDL_Social_Media folder in Dropbox and notify Madeline) with a caption for context to be shared on the lab instagram. Riley has also been creating reels so connect with him if you have video footage of your work!
